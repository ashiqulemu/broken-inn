APPETIZERS

SWEET CORN NUGGETS

We take delicious sweet corn and then and roll 'em into little balls, batter them and fry them in peanut oil. Served with our chipotle mayo sauce. Yummy and sweet enough to be a dessert...but it's an appetizer.

BAVARIAN PRETZELS

These things don't actually come from Bavaria, they come from the next best place... Restaurant Depot! HA! 4 soft pretzels, salted and served warm with both spicy mustard and our beer cheese sauce.

BONE-IN WINGS

Remember when wings were cheap? Sorry! 12 wings served with blue cheese and celery. Choose from mild, medium, hot, bbq, garlic parm or sweet chili sauces.

BONELESS WINGS

Cut from chicken breasts, not boneless chickens. That'd be weird. Served with blue cheese and celery. Same sauces as above.

COLOSSAL COCONUT SHRIMP

We make a certain amount each day and when they're gone... they're gone! Made from scratch using our family recipe and huge U6/8 shrimp (6-8 per pound) Served with a side of sweet chili sauce.

HUMMUS TO SHARE

Roasted red pepper hummus served with cucumbers, carrots, green and red peppers, kalamata olives and toasted pita points.

CAPRESE SALAD

Delicious and sharable! Mozzarella, tomatoes, fresh basil and our balsamic glaze. Pairs well with an enormous glass of wine!

FRENCH ONION SOUP

You know what this is. We don't need to describe it. And if you don't, Google it. Ours is like that... only better.

BURGERS

THE DELUXE

A delicious 8oz burger patty served medium rare or medium well with lettuce, tomato, onion and your choice of American, Swiss, provolone or cheddar cheeses on a brioche bun. Served with fries*. Add bacon for $2.

We like tasty things here and this burger is no different. Served with fries* Don't add bacon, it's already in there!

Named for the big guy and just a gluttonous offering. TWO 8oz burger patties, a slice of EACH of our cheeses (American, Swiss, provolone AND cheddar), an unhealthy amount of bacon, and NO veggies. Served with fries* Defibrillator sold separately.
