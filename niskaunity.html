PAST CONTRIBUTIONS

NISKAYUNA YOUTH BASEBALL & SOFTBALL
Funds raised were used to sponsor both Niskayuna youth baseball and Niskayuna youth softball teams!
NISKAYUNA COMMUNITY ACTION PROGRAM
Funds raised were used to help offset the costs of producing Niska-Day 2023!
NISKAYUNA COMMUNITY TRANSITION PROGRAM
Funds were used for activities such as cooking supplies, a train ride, YMCA membership, events at Proctors, and more!
ADOPT A FAMILY - NISKAYUNA
Funds raised on Santa's Guest Bartender night purchased Christmas Gifts for a Niskayuna family of seven.
NISKAYUNA LUMINARY DISPLAY
Funds were used to help offset the cost of the annual Niskayuna luminary display
NISKAYUNA GIRLS LACROSSE
Funds raised were used for an overnight trip to Suffern (paid for the hotel for the girls).
NISKAYUNA HIGH SCHOOL WEIGHT ROOM
Funds raised were used to fund the needs of the Niskayuna High School weight room.
NISKAYUNA SENIOR CENTER
Funds raised helped to cover the expense of the meal program offered as a part of the senior program here in the town.
NISKAYUNA SCHENECTADY GIRLS WRESTLING
Funds were used for tournament medals, start up funds for the tournament, food and drink items for concessions and finals singlets.
NISKY FRIENDS OF MUSIC
Funds raised helped support the District Choral Festival at Proctors, bringing together student singers from every Niskayuna school, grades 5-12.
NISKAYUNA YOUTH BASEBALL
Funds were put towards purchasing a new mower to cut the grass on the little league fields.
NISKAYUNA VARSITY FOOTBALL
Funds raised were used to purchase practice jerseys and travel track suits.
NEW YORK ONCOLOGY & HEMATOLOGY
Niskayuna's Chris Tocin & Gabby Calabrese raised funds for the "Rock Your Style Event" to support the New York Oncology and Hematology Foundation.
NISKAYUNA YOUTH FOOTBALL & CHEER
Funds were used towards cheer uniforms and tackle football jerseys and equipment (cheer mats, football helmets and shoulder pads).
MRS. NEW YORK AMERICAN
Funds were used toward postpartum resources for women in New York State.
NISKAYUNA STADIUM ENTRYWAY
Funds raised will go towards building the Entryway structure at the high school's new turf athletics field/stadium.
NISKAYUNA VARSITY BASEBALL
Funds raised helped defray the cost of their spring trip to Florida to practice and play scrimmages to prepare for the season.
MOHAWK'S HOCKEY TEAM
Funds were used towards purchasing a portable skate sharpener. They purchased a “Sparx” machine that is available to players daily.
